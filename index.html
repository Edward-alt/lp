Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about our gas subscription service

What exactly is a gas subscription?

It's a prepaid 3-month or 6-months plan that covers your monthly cooking gas refills. We pick up your cylinder, refill it and return it to you.

How does it work?

You choose a plan, Pay once, we schedule your refills monthly so you never run out. No surprise bills, no market wahala.

Do you offer one-time refills?

No. We only server customers on our subscription plans. This helps us maintain speed, convenience and better pricing for you.

Can i pay monthly instead?

No. We only offer 3-months and 6-months prepaid plans. This protects you from price increase and gives better service value.

Is delivery free?

Delivery is included in your subscription fee. No extra payment every time. No hidden charges.

What if i run out of gas before my next refill?

You get fast tracked refill support, we prioritize our subscribers for urgent deliveries if your usage goes up unexpectedly.

What cylinder sizes do you support?

We currently handle 10kg to 100kg, including 12.5kg for homes and custom plans for restaurants, offices and bulk user.

Do I need to own a cylinder?

Yes. Currently, we do not provide gas cylinders, you use yours-we reill and return.

What if i move houses?

If your new location is within our coverage, we'll update your details and continue service- no interruption.

Do you have plans for restaurants or buisnesses?

Yes. We offer custom bulk refil plans for buisnesses and food vendors. Let us know your average monthly usage and we'll tailor a plan specially for you.

Can I gift someone a gas plan?

Yes. We offer Las Palmas Gas Gift Cards. Perfect for families, friends, newlyweds or elderly parents.

What do i loose by not subscribing?

If you buy gas only when it finishes: You may run out mid-cooking or late at night, you'll likely pay ₦300 -₦500 more per kg. You pay ₦2000 - ₦3500 per refill delivery on average. You repeat this stress 12+ times a year. Average yearly loss: ₦70,000 - ₦80,000 for homes, for restaurants: up to ₦150,000 ₦250,000 lost yearly.
With Las Palams, you lock your price, skip the stress and save big while enjoying professional delivery.
