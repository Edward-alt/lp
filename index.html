Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gas subscription service

How does the automatic refill system work?

Our system estimates your gas usage based on your plan and household size. As you approach your refill date, we automatically schedule a delivery. You'll receive WhatsApp notifications about your upcoming delivery and can reschedule if needed.

What happens if I'm not home during delivery?

Our delivery team will contact you before arrival. If you won't be home, you can either: 1) Reschedule the delivery, 2) Authorize us to leave the cylinder with a neighbor/security, or 3) Provide safe storage instructions. We always prioritize safety and security.

Can I change or upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at your next billing cycle. For immediate upgrades, we prorate the difference. Downgrades may require completing your current billing period depending on your plan terms.

How do you ensure cylinder safety?

All our cylinders undergo rigorous safety checks before delivery. Our trained technicians perform leak tests during installation and provide safety guidelines. We include free safety stickers with each delivery and offer optional annual safety inspections for long-term subscribers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major debit/credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. For corporate accounts, we can arrange invoice billing. All payments are processed through secure platforms with encryption for your protection.

Do you service my area?

We currently serve all major cities in Nigeria including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, Kano, and Benin. During registration, simply enter your address to confirm coverage. We're expanding rapidly, so if we're not in your area yet, join our waitlist for priority access when we launch.
